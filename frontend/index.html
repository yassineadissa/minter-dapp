About the project

Pixiborg Crypto is a NFT collection of 5000 unique cyborgs who live in the planet PX-69 on the Ethereum Blockchain.
Each Pixiborg is completely unique based on a multitude of possible traits and features.
All Pixiborgs feature our own hand-drawn in pixel art.
Each Pixiborg is randomly generated and is designed by the way it sees its residual self-image.
Your Cyber-Pixi NFT does not only serve you as an artwork but it becomes your entry ticket to PX-69.
Becoming a part of the Cyber-Pixi Family means you join a family of people who have great belief in Web 3.0, Cryptocurrencies and the Blockchain technology.
